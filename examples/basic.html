A short blog post with a 3D model in it.

This page shows what an inline Blauw Viewer embed looks like from a reader's perspective.

We open with a paragraph of body copy, because nothing dates a blog post faster than a page that's nothing but media. Readers need context and rhythm before the hero moment arrives.

Then a second paragraph continues the piece. The 3D viewer above is fully interactive — click to load, drag to orbit, scroll to zoom, enter fullscreen if you want to get immersive about it.

Everything inside the viewer lives in a shadow DOM, so nothing that Webflow does to the surrounding article can style it, and nothing the viewer does can style the rest of the page. It's self-contained by design.
