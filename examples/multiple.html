Three embeds on one page.

Each viewer is its own shadow-DOM instance with its own canvas. The engine chunk (Three.js) is only fetched once across all of them.

Default — with title and author

Auto-rotate, no on-screen UI

data-no-ui="true" strips the control bar entirely — good for hero placements where you don't want chrome.

Transparent background

data-transparent="true" lets whatever's behind the viewer show through the frame. Useful on non-default page backgrounds.
